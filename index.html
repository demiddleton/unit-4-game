Crystals Collector!

Instructions:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • By clicking on one of the four crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  • Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the target number and you lose the game if you total score goes above the target number.
